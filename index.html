Ingredient Spotlight: Starfruit

Starfruit, scientifically known as carambola, is a captivating tropical fruit cherished for both its unique appearance and versatile culinary applications. When sliced crosswise, the starfruit's flesh forms a striking star shape, which not only adds a visual flourish to dishes but also offers a delightful blend of sweet and tangy flavors. This exotic fruit can be enjoyed in various ways: it can be eaten fresh as a crisp and juicy snack, added to fruit salads for a burst of flavor, or juiced for a refreshing beverage. It's also a popular garnish for desserts, cocktails, and seafood dishes, lending a tropical touch to culinary creations. Beyond its culinary appeal, starfruit is packed with vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants, making it a healthy addition to one's diet. Its versatility and exotic charm have made starfruit a favored ingredient in both sweet and savory recipes, allowing food enthusiasts to savor a taste of the tropics.

Star Fruit Salad

This delightful Star Fruit Salad is a refreshing and exotic blend of flavors and textures. Star fruit, also known as carambola, takes center stage with its unique, star-shaped slices that add a touch of elegance to the salad. The star fruit is combined with tropical fruits like mango and kiwi, offering a harmonious balance of sweet and tangy. A zesty lime-honey dressing drizzled over the fruits enhances their natural sweetness, and a sprinkle of fresh mint leaves provides a burst of freshness. It's a perfect salad to enjoy on a warm day, and it can be served as a light dessert or a side dish at any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe star fruits (carambola), sliced
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled, pitted, and cubed
  • 2 kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the star fruit slices, mango cubes, and kiwi slices.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime zest, lime juice, and honey to create the dressing.
  3. Drizzle the lime-honey dressing over the fruit mixture and gently toss to coat.
  4. Garnish the salad with fresh mint leaves.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant flavors of this star fruit salad.

Cinnamon StarFruit Tart

This dessert makes the starfruit shine in an unusual elegant way, and best of all its super easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 starfruit, thinly sliced
  • ice cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400. Line a baking sheet with parchment or a silicone liner.
  2. Unfold the puff pastry sheet when it's thawed but still cool and smooth out the creases and press them together.
  3. Whisk the egg and brush it over the pastry sheet.
  4. Mix the sugar, cinnamon, allspice, and salt in a small bowl. Sprinkle half the cinnamon sugar over the pastry. Arrange the starfruit slices over the top. Sprinkle with the rest of the cinnamon sugar.
  5. Bake for about 25 minutes, or until the tart is puffed and golden brown.
  6. Allow the tart to cool at least 5 minutes before serving. Top each slice with a scoop of ice cream if you like.

Star Fruit and Mint Lemonade

This Star Fruit and Mint Lemonade is perfect for a hot day or as a delightful beverage for gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe star fruits (carambola), sliced
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4-5 fresh mint leaves
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes for serving
  • Mint sprigs and star fruit slices for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash the star fruits thoroughly.
  2. Slice the star fruits crosswise to create star-shaped slices. Remove any seeds.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine 1/2 cup of water, sugar, and fresh mint leaves.
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Once the sugar is dissolved, remove the syrup from heat and let it cool. Strain to remove mint leaves.
  6. In a pitcher, combine the fresh lemon juice and cold water.
  7. Add the mint syrup to the lemonade base and stir well. Adjust the sweetness according to your preference.
  8. Add the sliced star fruits to the lemonade pitcher. Stir gently to incorporate the star fruit slices.
  9. Refrigerate the lemonade for at least 1-2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  10. When ready to serve, fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the star fruit and mint lemonade over the ice.
  11. Garnish each glass with a sprig of mint and a star fruit slice for a decorative touch.
