Cooking Terms

  • Saute

    To fry food quickly in a small amount of oil or fat over relatively high heat.

  • Braise

    To cook meat or vegetables by browning in fat, then simmering in a covered dish with a small amount of liquid.

  • Zest

    The grated outer peel of citrus fruits, used as flavoring.

  • Blanch

    To partially cook fruits, vegetables, or nuts in boiling water or steam.

  • Al dente

    Italian term meaning 'to the tooth' - usually refers to pasta that is cooked to be slightly firm when bitten.

  • Mirepoix

    A mix of onions, carrots, and celery used as a base for soups, stews, and sauces.
